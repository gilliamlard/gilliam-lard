# Gilliam Lard Website — Design Brainstorm

## Context
Personal real estate website for a young, tech-savvy, faith-driven real estate advisor. Primary reference: landonorris.com (bold, personality-driven, modern animations). Color palette: dark/black backgrounds, white text, maroon/burgundy accent. Lead capture via SMS is the primary goal.

## Idea 1: "Noir Monolith" — Brutalist Luxury

**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalist meets luxury editorial, inspired by high-end fashion lookbooks and architectural portfolios.

**Core Principles:**
1. Stark contrast — near-pure black (#0A0A0A) against crisp white, with a single maroon accent (#6B1D2A) used surgically
2. Oversized typography as architecture — text itself becomes the visual structure
3. Deliberate asymmetry — nothing centered, everything offset with intention
4. Negative space as a luxury signal

**Color Philosophy:** The darkness communicates authority and sophistication. Maroon is used only at moments of action (CTAs, hover states, accent lines) — it represents warmth breaking through the professional exterior, mirroring Gilliam's faith-driven warmth beneath a polished exterior.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport sections with dramatic left-aligned or split-screen compositions. The hero is a full-bleed headshot on one side with oversized stacked text on the other. Sections use alternating asymmetric grids — text heavy-left with imagery right, then reversed.

**Signature Elements:**
1. A persistent floating SMS CTA pill that follows scroll — maroon with white text, subtle pulse animation
2. Thin horizontal maroon accent lines that animate in on scroll, dividing sections
3. Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) in a thin weight, positioned as watermarks behind content

**Interaction Philosophy:** Minimal but precise. Hover states reveal subtle maroon underlines. The SMS button has a magnetic cursor effect. Scroll reveals are clean clip-path animations — text slides up from behind invisible masks.

**Animation:** Staggered text reveals using clip-path translateY. Sections fade in with a slight parallax offset. The headshot has a subtle Ken Burns zoom on load. Maroon accent lines draw themselves across the viewport on scroll entry.

**Typography System:** Display: "Instrument Serif" (elegant, editorial) for the name and section headers. Body: "Inter" or "Geist" at 300-400 weight for clean readability. Contrast between serif display and sans-serif body creates the "preppy meets tech" tension.

## Idea 2: "Digital Gentleman" — Swiss Modernism with Southern Warmth

**Design Movement:** International Typographic Style (Swiss Design) reinterpreted through a warm, personality-driven lens — think Dieter Rams meets a Virginia gentleman's study.

**Core Principles:**
1. Grid discipline with human warmth — strict underlying grid but with organic photography and warm accent colors softening the rigidity
2. Information hierarchy through scale — massive type for emotional impact, refined type for details
3. Motion as personality — animations feel confident and unhurried, like a firm handshake
4. Every element earns its place

**Color Philosophy:** Primary black (#0C0C0C) as the canvas of authority. Off-white (#F5F2ED) for text — slightly warm, not clinical. Burgundy (#7A2036) as the accent of action and passion. A subtle dark charcoal (#1A1A1A) for card backgrounds, creating depth within the dark palette.

**Layout Paradigm:** Vertical rhythm driven by a strong baseline grid. The hero section uses a dramatic full-height split — headshot occupying 55% width with text stacked vertically on the remaining 45%. Below, content flows in a single-column editorial style with generous margins, punctuated by full-bleed burgundy accent sections.

**Signature Elements:**
1. A "Text Me" button that's impossibly large in the hero — taking up significant real estate, impossible to miss, with a phone icon and arrow
2. Subtle grain texture overlay on the dark background — adds tactile quality
3. Monospaced metadata labels (like "SERVICE AREA" or "PHILOSOPHY") in small caps above content blocks

**Interaction Philosophy:** Confident and deliberate. Buttons have a satisfying scale-up on hover with a burgundy glow. Scroll-triggered animations are smooth and directional — content enters from the direction you'd naturally read. The SMS CTA has a subtle shake animation every 8 seconds to draw attention.

**Animation:** Content blocks animate in with a combination of opacity fade and translateY (30px). The headshot has a reveal animation — a burgundy overlay slides away to reveal the photo. Section transitions use a subtle parallax effect. Typography animates word-by-word for the tagline.

**Typography System:** Display: "Syne" (geometric, modern, confident) for headers and the name. Body: "DM Sans" at 400 weight for warmth and readability. Metadata/labels: "JetBrains Mono" or "Space Mono" in small caps for a tech-forward detail layer.

## Idea 3: "Velocity" — Motion-First Editorial

**Design Movement:** Kinetic typography meets editorial design — inspired directly by landonorris.com's energy and bold personality, adapted for real estate with sophistication.

**Core Principles:**
1. Motion is the medium — every element has a considered entrance, and the page feels alive
2. Bold scale shifts — massive headlines next to refined body text create visual drama
3. Dark canvas, bright moments — the dark background makes every light element feel intentional and precious
4. The CTA is the hero — the SMS button is not an afterthought, it's the architectural centerpiece

**Color Philosophy:** Deep black (#080808) as the infinite canvas. Pure white (#FFFFFF) for primary text — maximum contrast, maximum readability. Rich maroon (#8B1A2B) used generously but strategically — as button fills, section accents, and hover states. A warm gray (#9A9A9A) for secondary text. The maroon is the "heartbeat" color — it appears at every moment of human connection (CTA, about section, contact).

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-viewport stacked sections with dramatic scale. The hero is a full-screen dark canvas with the headshot as a large circular or rounded-rectangle element, flanked by oversized stacked text. Below, sections alternate between full-width text moments and contained grid layouts. A horizontal scrolling ticker/marquee element adds the landonorris.com energy.

**Signature Elements:**
1. A marquee/ticker strip with repeating text ("TEXT ME • FAITHFUL SERVICE • YOUR FUTURE •") in maroon, scrolling horizontally — adds energy and personality
2. The headshot presented with a dramatic rounded crop and a subtle maroon border glow
3. Stacked oversized text where each line animates in separately with a slight delay

**Interaction Philosophy:** Everything responds. Buttons scale and shift color on hover. The page feels like it's breathing — subtle background gradient shifts. The SMS CTA has a bold hover state with a slide-in arrow icon. Cursor interactions feel magnetic near interactive elements.

**Animation:** Hero text animates in line-by-line with a staggered clip-path reveal. The headshot scales up from 90% to 100% with a slight rotation correction. Scroll-triggered sections use intersection observer with translateY + opacity. The marquee runs continuously. Section headers have a character-by-character reveal. Smooth scroll behavior throughout.

**Typography System:** Display: "Space Grotesk" (geometric, techy, bold) for the name and major headers — captures the "graphic design tech nerd" energy. Body: "Inter" at 400 weight for clean readability. Accent: "Space Grotesk" at 300 weight for subtle labels. The contrast between bold geometric headers and clean body text creates the modern-yet-approachable feel.
