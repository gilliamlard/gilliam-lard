# Research: What Makes Real Estate Websites Attractive to Home Buyers

## Key Findings:

### Color Psychology for Real Estate:
- Blue = Trust, professionalism, reliability (industry favorite)
- Green = Growth, harmony, fresh starts
- Warm neutrals = Stability, comfort, "home" feeling
- White = Clean, spacious, modern
- Accent colors (warm tones) = Action, urgency for CTAs

### Trust-Building Elements:
- Consistent branding throughout
- Professional photography
- Social proof (reviews, testimonials) - CRITICAL
- Clear contact information prominently displayed
- Personal bio/story that humanizes the agent

### Design Best Practices:
- Mobile-first (most buyers browse on phones)
- Fast loading times
- Easy navigation
- High-quality imagery
- Warm, inviting color palette (NOT dark/intimidating)
- Clean, uncluttered layout
- Strong CTAs that stand out

## Design Direction for Gilliam's Redesign:
- Shift from dark/black to WARM, BRIGHT, INVITING
- Use cream/warm white backgrounds instead of black
- Keep maroon/burgundy as accent but make it warmer
- Add halation/glow effects (warm light leaks, soft glows)
- Use warm golden tones for the glow effects
- Maintain elegant typography (Space Grotesk)
- Add testimonials section with Google reviews
- Make it feel like "home" - warm, trustworthy, personal
